About IPATE Deliveries

Our Mission

At IPATE Deliveries, our mission is to revolutionize last-mile delivery in Nairobi by providing fast, reliable, and affordable delivery services to both individuals and businesses.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, IPATE Deliveries was born out of a need to solve the growing delivery challenges in Nairobi's bustling urban environment. We noticed that many businesses and individuals struggled with timely and cost-effective delivery solutions.

Our platform connects customers with verified riders, ensuring that packages reach their destinations safely and on time. We leverage technology to optimize routes, track deliveries in real-time, and provide exceptional customer service.

Our Values

  • Reliability: We deliver on our promises, every time
  • Speed: We understand that time is of the essence
  • Safety: We prioritize the security of both our riders and your packages
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our technology and processes
  • Community: We support local businesses and create economic opportunities
